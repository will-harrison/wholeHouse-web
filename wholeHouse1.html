wholeHouse

One app to rule them all.

Why do we need so many apps? Why do we have 17 ways to send our friends a message? Why are there so many unintuitive steps to view my kids' calendar? wholeHouse combines user information, notifications, location, time, and lists - and connects them in an intuitive experience.

One platform for all devices.

My phone doesn't know anything about my tablet, that doesn't know anything about my desktop computer, that doesn't know anything about my work computer, my TV, my car, my ... wholeHouse is a central server that all of your devices view in real time. Now, your phone, table, computer, and eventually TV, car, alarm clock... are in sync - all the time, without your intervention.

That lives on your own private cloud.

We can host a very inexpensive, private, secure virual machine - if you have no idea what I just said. Or you can host it yourself. I'm developing on a raspberry pi on my shelf. The point is that your data is yours. I don't want it, I don't want to sell it.
